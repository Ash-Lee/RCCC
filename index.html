Welcome to the Rotherham Cancer Care Centre

The Rotherham Cancer Care Centre is a local charity dedicated to improving the quality of life for people living with cancer in and around the Rotherham Borough.

We provide emotional support through counselling and complimentary therapies. Our services are free of charge and we rely on donations from supporters and help from volunteers to continue our work.
